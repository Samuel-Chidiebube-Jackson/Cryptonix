Psalm 23

The Lord is my shepherd, I shall not want, He makes me to lie down in green pastures,he leads me beside the still waters, for His name sake, even though I walk through the value of the shadow of death, I shall fear no evil, for thou art me, thy rock and thy staff they comfort me, thou preparedst a table before me in the presence of my enemies, thou anointedst my head with oil, my cup overflows,surely, goodness and mercies shall follow me,all the days of my life, and I shall dwell in the house of the Lord, forever and ever, Amen!!!
